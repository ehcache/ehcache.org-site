Ehcache 3 : Milestone 4 reached - Even more features

Milestone 4 came with over 30 issues closed and includes new features, such as:
  • Simplified and reworked write-behind implementation,
  • OSGi readiness,
  • Ability to pass instances of CacheLoaderWriter, Serializer and Copier when configuring a Cache,
  • See all closed issues
